    The quartic geometric crux: the partial defect leaves every digit window #

    Degree-4 analogue of CubicFinish.cubicGpd_exceeds_window. For any schedule (c₀,c₁,c₂) and any constant C, there is an interior non-jump point (r₁,r₂,r₃,r₄) ∈ (0,1)⁴ where the three-floor partial defect quarticGpd = α³fA + α²fB + α·fC (with fA,fB,fC ∈ (0,1)) leaves the width-2 window (C−2, C]. The range (0, α³+α²+α) has width α³+α²+α > 3 > 2 (as α = 2^{1/4} > 1), so no window of length 2 contains it. Realization reuses CubicFinish.fract_shift_realize: fix r₁ = 1/2, solve r₂,r₃,r₄ for the three prescribed fractional parts.
