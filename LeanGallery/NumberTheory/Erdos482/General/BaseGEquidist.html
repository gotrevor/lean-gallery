    a.e. equidistribution of the base-g orbit {gⁿ s} #

    The base-g (g ≥ 2) analogue of DoublingEquidist. For almost every real s, the orbit n ↦ ↑(gⁿ s) is equidistributed on ℝ/ℤ — the base-g Borel normality statement, via the Davenport–Erdős–LeVeque L² method (BaseGWeyl.baseG_weyl_L2_normalized + the DEL engine).

    • ae_baseG_weyl_tendsto / ae_baseG_weyl_tendsto_real: per-frequency a.e. vanishing of the base-g Weyl average (over [0,1] resp. all of ℝ).
    • ae_baseG_orbit_equidistributed / _real: a.e. equidistribution of the base-g orbit.

    The general periodicity bridge ae_of_ae_restrict_Icc01_of_periodic and the gap-fill / Weyl-criterion / DEL pieces are all base-agnostic and reused verbatim.
