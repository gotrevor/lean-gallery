    The q-Padé denominator pₙ and its integrality (toward Borwein Lemma 2) #

    pVal n is the real value of Borwein's denominator polynomial pₙ(c,q) at c = 8/3, q = 2 (second form, via Gaussian binomials). pInt n is the 3^{n-1}-cleared integer version. The lemma pInt_cast (Borwein Lemma 2, integrality half) says they agree after clearing — so 3^{n-1}·pₙ ∈ ℤ.

    This is a prerequisite for discharging borwein_integrality (O1); the remaining piece is the residue identity (Lemma 1, see ON-LINE-REQUEST.md).
