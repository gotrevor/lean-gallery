    Denominator integrality (Borwein Lemma 2) + the q-Lagrange identity #

    Part of the now-complete, axiom-clean proof of Erdős #1050 (#print axioms erdos_1050 = [propext, Classical.choice, Quot.sound]). This file machine-checks Borwein Lemma 2 (integrality of the q-Padé denominator pₙ) and pins the approximant to the actual Padé denominator pVal: the coefficient β^{2n}·Wₙ·pVal n is the PROVEN integer Bden n (Bden_cast), built from pInt (Lemma 2 via the Cauchy q-binomial theorem qBin_cauchy) and the 3-power / 2-power clearings of Wₙ. It also derives qLag_thm (the q-Lagrange identity at q = 2) from qLagrange (QLagrange.lean).

    The downstream chain borwein_integrality → irrational_zB → erdos_1050 lives in Lemma3.lean (after the elementary Lemma-3 machinery, which is what made the whole proof axiom-clean).
