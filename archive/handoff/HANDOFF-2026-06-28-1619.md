## ⚠️ Gotchas
- **"Too many open files" on a wide cold `lake build` is NOT the container fd limit.** The box sets
  `--ulimit nofile=1048576` (in effect, confirmed) but a `lean` process importing Mathlib holds ~12 fds
  and **~42k file-backed mmaps**; `nproc=16` workers → ~680k mmaps against the bind-mount → exceeds the
  macOS host (`kern.maxfiles`) via OrbStack virtiofs, surfacing as EMFILE in-container. **Workaround:
  build in small batches (≤4 targets).** `lake build` has no `-j`/`--jobs` flag in this version.
- **`#print axioms` wraps long names** past ~100 cols into 3 lines → breaks single-line `#guard_msgs`
  docstrings (rewrite them wrapped) and grep-based CI gates (gate short names only). `set_option
  format.width` does not fix it.
- **Cached `lake build` won't re-emit infos/linters** — to verify warning/info-cleanliness you must
  force a fresh recompile (clear oleans), not trust an incremental no-op.
