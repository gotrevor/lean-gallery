    A binary product of finite simplicial sets is finite #

    If X₁ and X₂ are respectively of dimensions ≤ d₁ and ≤ d₂, then X₁ ⊗ X₂ has dimension ≤ d₁ + d₂.

    We also show that if X₁ and X₂ are finite, then X₁ ⊗ X₂ is also finite.
