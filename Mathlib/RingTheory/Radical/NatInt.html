    The radical in ℕ and ℤ #

    Declarations for ℕ #

    • UniqueFactorizationMonoid.primeFactors_eq_natPrimeFactors: The prime factors of a natural number are the same as the prime factors defined in Nat.primeFactors.
    • Nat.radical_eq_prod_primeFactors: The radical is computable for natural numbers.
    • Nat.radical_le_self_iff: if n ≠ 0, radical n ≤ n.
    • Nat.two_le_radical_iff: 2 ≤ n.radical iff 2 ≤ n.

    Declarations for ℤ #

    • UniqueFactorizationMonoid.primeFactors_eq_primeFactors_natAbs: The prime factors of an integer are the same as the prime factors of its absolute value.
    • Int.radical_eq_prod_primeFactors: The radical is computable for integers.
