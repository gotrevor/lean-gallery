    Skyscraper sheaves #

    Let Φ be a point of a site (C, J). In this file, we construct the skyscraper sheaf functor skyscraperSheafFunctor : A ⥤ Sheaf J A and show that it is a right adjoint to Φ.sheafFiber : Sheaf J A ⥤ A.
