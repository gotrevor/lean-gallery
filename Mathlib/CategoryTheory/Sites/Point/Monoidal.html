    Fiber functors are monoidal #

    Let Φ be a point of a site (C, J). Let A be a monoidal category where the tensor product commutes with filtered colimits in both variables. We show that the fiber functors Φ.presheafFiber : (Cᵒᵖ ⥤ A) ⥤ A and Φ.sheafFiber : Sheaf J A ⥤ A are monoidal.
