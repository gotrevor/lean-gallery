    Parametrized adjunctions and limits #

    Given bifunctors F : C₁ ⥤ C₂ ⥤ C₃, G : C₁ᵒᵖ ⥤ C₃ ⥤ C₂ and a parametrized adjunction adj₂ : F ⊣₂ G, we show that for any X₃ : C₃, the functor G.flip.obj X₃ : C₁ᵒᵖ ⥤ C₃ preserves limits of shape J if for any X₂ : C₂, the functor F.flip.obj X₂ : C₁ ⥤ C₃ preserves colimits of shape Jᵒᵖ.
