    Closure of a property of objects under limits of certain shapes #

    In this file, given a property P of objects in a category C and a family of categories J : α → Type _, we introduce the closure P.limitsClosure J of P under limits of shapes J a for all a : α, and under certain smallness assumptions, we show that it is essentially small.
