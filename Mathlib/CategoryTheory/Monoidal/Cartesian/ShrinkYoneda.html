    The Yoneda embedding for monoid objects for locally small categories #

    Let C be a locally w-small category. We define the Yoneda embedding shrinkYonedaMon : Mon C ⥤ Cᵒᵖ ⥤ MonCat.{w} w and its Grp analogue.
