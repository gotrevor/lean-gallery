    LineGraph #

    Main definitions #

    • SimpleGraph.lineGraph is the line graph of a simple graph G, with vertices as the edges of G and two vertices of the line graph adjacent if the corresponding edges share a vertex in G.

    def SimpleGraph.lineGraph {V : Type u_1} (G : SimpleGraph V) :
    SimpleGraph ↑G.edgeSet

    The line graph of a simple graph G has its vertex set as the edges of G, and two vertices of the line graph are adjacent if the corresponding edges share a vertex in G.
