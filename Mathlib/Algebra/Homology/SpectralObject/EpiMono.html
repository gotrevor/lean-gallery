    Induced morphisms that are epi or mono #

    Given a spectral object in an abelian category, we show that certain morphisms E^n(f₁, f₂, f₃) ⟶ E^n(f₁', f₂', f₃') are monomorphisms, epimorphisms or isomorphisms.
