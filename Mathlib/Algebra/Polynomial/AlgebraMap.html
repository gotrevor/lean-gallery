    Theory of univariate polynomials #

    We show that A[X] is an R-algebra when A is an R-algebra. We promote eval₂ to an algebra hom in aeval.

    Main definitions #

    • Polynomial.aeval: given a valuation x of the variable in an R-algebra A, aeval R A x is the unique R-algebra homomorphism from R[X] to A sending X to x.

    • Polynomial.mapAlgHom : given φ : S →ₐ[R] S', mapAlgHom φ applies φ on the coefficients of a polynomial in S[X].
